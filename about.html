About us


Founded amidst the vibrant era of the 1970s, our jewelry brand emerged as a beacon of creativity and sophistication. What began as a modest venture blossomed into a global phenomenon, captivating hearts worldwide.

Through the decades, our commitment to exquisite craftsmanship and timeless design has fueled our ascent to international acclaim. From the eclectic influences of the '70s to the cutting-edge styles of today, our collections weave a rich tapestry of aesthetic evolution.

Each piece, a testament to our enduring legacy, reflects not only the trends of the moment but also the enduring allure that transcends time. As a globally celebrated brand, our jewelry has become synonymous with luxury, adorned by discerning individuals across continents.

With an unwavering dedication to quality and innovation, our journey from the 1970s to the present day stands as a testament to the enduring power of artistic expression in the world of fine jewelry

Where we started

In the middle of the town, the first shop of White Swan, a vintage jewelry brand, now looks all quiet and closed. This old place, with its tired front and shut windows, feels a bit sad and full of memories.

The pretty designs on the outside, not so bright anymore, tell stories from a long time ago. Even though the door is locked, you can almost hear the sounds of people who used to come in.

Behind the dusty glass, the inside, once filled with shiny jewels, seems stuck in the past. White Swan' first shop, now not open, holds on to old stories and moments that slipped away.

Now

Shining bright in the success spotlight, White Swan Jewelry has become a big name in the jewelry world.We've won cool awards for our awesome designs and are known for using materials that are good for the planet.

White Swan isn't just about making pretty jewelry, we've also made a name for themselves around the world. From the fancy gems we use to the special way we craft each piece, White Swan is all about doing things with style and care.

Our success is like a story told by each piece of jewelry, showing off not just beauty, but a commitment to making the world of jewelry a bit more special
